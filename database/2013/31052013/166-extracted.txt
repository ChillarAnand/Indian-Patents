(57) Abstract :
Method of fabricating a plastic part decorated with relief The invention relates to a method (1) for fabricating a part (21) with
relief wherein the portions in relief (23 25 27) are at least partially coated with a decorative layer (31) including the following
step: a) forming (3) the body (22) of the part (21); According to the invention the method further includes the following steps: b)
selectively altering (5) the surface state of the body (22) of the part (21) in order to change the adherence thereof locally relative to
said decorative layer; c) directly depositing (7) said decorative layer over the entire body (22); d) removing (9) the portions of the
deposited layer (31) which have not adhered to the body (22) of the part. The invention concerns the field of methods for
decorating timepieces.
