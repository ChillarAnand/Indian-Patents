(57) Abstract :
The present invention provides a novel process for the preparation of iloperidone using a novel intermediate. Thus for example,
reacting 4-(3-chloropropoxy)-3-methoxy benzaldehyde with methyl magnesium iodide in ether and the reaction mass was heated
for 6 hours at reflux temperature, the mass was cooled to ambient temperature and poured into a mixture of ice, water and dilute
hydrochloric acid to obtain l-[4-(3-chloropropoxy)-3-methoxyphenyl]ethanol.
