(57) Abstract :
The present invention relates to a novel system of phytase supplementation in aquaculture. The invention further relates to a novel
system of phytase supplementation in aquaculture, which ensures thermal stability and acid tolerance during feed pelleting and as
well ensuring easy recovery of unconsumed phytase from aquaculture in a closed water system.
