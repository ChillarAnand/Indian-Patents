(57) Abstract :
The present invention relates to a method for the preparation of mechano-structured nacre by mechanosynthesis of micrometric
nacre powder characterized in that the temperature of the nacre is kept below 40°C. It also relates to said mechano-structured
nacre and to uses thereof in particular on implants and bone substitute pieces on which the mechano-structured nacre is deposited.
