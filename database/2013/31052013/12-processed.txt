Abstract  =>  The present invention generally relates to the preparation and evaluation of standardized solid dosage of Trikatu. More particularly, it relates to advanced dosages formulations of Trikatu, which is a well known an Ayurvedic preparation. The formulations are developed in the form of tablets and dispersible tablets. To develop this preparation, the extract of Trikatu ingredients was standardized and formulated in the form of tablets using direct compression technique.
