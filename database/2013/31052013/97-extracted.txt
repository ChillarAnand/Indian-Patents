(57) Abstract :
A fluid pumping system for a vehicle having an internal combustion engine includes a housing an electric motor a controller to
control the speed of the electric motor a planetary gearset including a first member adapted to be driven by the internal
combustion engine a second member driven by the electric motor and a third member. A pump is driven by the third member of
the planetary gearset. The housing contains the electric motor the pump and the controller.
