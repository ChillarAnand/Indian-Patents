A method for detecting incorrect filling of a tank for an aqueous urea solution including the following steps: Measuring a
temperature of a fluid in the tank, heating of the fluid in the tank, determining a value representing a rate of temperature change
of the fluid in the tank, comparing the value to a reference value.
