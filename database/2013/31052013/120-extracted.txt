(57) Abstract :
An inkjet printing system includes a print head in fluid communication with an ink reservoir and having a plurality of orifices and
a corresponding plurality of associated ejection chambers. The print head includes a substrate and a barrier layer disposed on the
substrate. The barrier layer defines in part a plurality of fluid channels and the plurality of ejection chambers. The barrier layer
includes a material selected from epoxy-based photo resist materials and methyl methacrylate-based photo resist materials. An
orifice plate is disposed over the substrate. The orifice plate includes the plurality of orifices in fluid communication with the
ejection chambers. The orifice plate comprises a material selected from polyirnides and nickel.
