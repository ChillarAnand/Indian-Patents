(57) Abstract : 
A sealing system for the cylinder head valve seat pocket to valve seat insert interface of a Cylinder head assembly comprises a valve 
seat pocket gasket compressed between a valve seat insert pocket and a valve seat insert having an active layer of material with at least 
one seal, defining a compression seal at the interface of the seal and the valve seat insert pocket, the valve seat insert or both, to 
thereby prevent the ingress of fuel or water between the valve seat insert and the valve seat insert pocket.
