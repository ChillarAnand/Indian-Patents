(57) Abstract : 
Embodiments of the invention are directed to systems, methods, and computer program products configured to calculate an indicator 
of the likelihood that an entitlement exists in a first community relative to a second community. The calculated indicator is then used 
to determine the appropriateness of entitlements within the first community or after a transfer of a person from the first community to 
the second.
