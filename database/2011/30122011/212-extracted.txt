(57) Abstract : 
The present invention relates to a laundry composition comprising 0.5 to 20 wt% hydroxamate and 5 to 80 wt% of a surfactant system 
comprising anionic and nonionic surfactant in a ratio of from 1 :1.1 to 19:1. The composition exhibits enhanced detergency, especially 
in relation to particulate stains.
