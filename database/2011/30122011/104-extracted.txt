The purpose of this invention is to present a device for utilisation of the horizontal component of wave motions in which there is no 
proportionality between the installed power and the initial costs of the device. According to the invention, this is obtained with a 
device which contains at least one oblong supporting structure and three or more independently movable resistance elements.
