(57) Abstract : 
Converged network management application and system is provided that delivers a management platform as a service that can view 
and/or manage all managed networks in the aggregate, or any one of them individually (including individual devices within the 
managed networks), in a secure and efficient manner, providing continuously available intelligence in real time on the managed 
networks and systems, and overcoming integration issues including conflicting address schemas, the need to avoid unnecessary 
infrastructure, and the need acquire all necessary information in real time within applicable memory and bandwidth constraints.
