(57) Abstract : 
This invention relates to a drive unit for driving a ring gear, comprising a motor, a transmission and a drive shaft, on which a pinion is 
arranged for driving the ring gear, wherein the drive unit includes a mechanical overload protection. In accordance with the invention 
the overload protection is arranged between the transmission and the pinion.
