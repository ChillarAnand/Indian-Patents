(57) Abstract : 
The present invention provides the prasugrel bisulfate of formula (II) and pharmaceutical composition and use thereof. Prasugrel 
bisulfate of the present invention has good stability, oral absorbability, metabolic activity and platelet aggregation inhibition effect, 
and low toxicity, and is therefore a promising anticoagulant for preventing or treating diseases associated with thrombosis or 
embolism.
