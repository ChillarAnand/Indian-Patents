(57) Abstract : 
A kit for preparing a cell concentrate from a physiological solution, comprising a first filtration device and a second filtration device, 
both of which are housed in a suitable container, such that after filtration, the cell concentrate comprises platelets and nucleated cells 
per unit volume greater than in the physiological solution.
