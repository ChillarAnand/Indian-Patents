(57) Abstract : 
Methods and devices useful, for example, in the field of angioplasty and stenting are disclosed. In some embodiments, the methods, 
devices and kits are configured for directional expansion inside a lumen, for example of a blood vessel obstructed by plaque. In some 
embodiments, the directional expansion displaces the plaque in a desired direction.
