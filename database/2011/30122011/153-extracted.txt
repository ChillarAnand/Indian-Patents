(57) Abstract : 
Gel prevention agents and methods for use with agricultural products include certain alkylamidoamines and their quaternary 
ammonium counterparts that, when blended with products containing alkoxylated surfactants, the alkylamidoamines and their 
quaternary counterparts reduce the gel formation tendency of such products.
