(57) Abstract : 
The present invention provides an improved process for the preparation of prasugrel and its 
pharmaceutical  acceptable  salt.  Prasugrel  chemically  known  as  2-acetoxy-5-(α-cyclopropylcarbonyl-2-fluorobenzyl)-4,5,6,7-
tetrahydrothieno[3,2-c]-pyridine    or   5-[2-cyclopropyl-l-(2-fluorophenyl)-2-oxoethyl]-4,5,6,7-tetrahydrothieno[3,2,-c]pyridine-2yl 
acetate and having the structural formula. and its pharmaceutically acceptable salts. The present invention also provides an improved 
process for the preparation of cyclopropyl 2-fluorobenzyl ketone, 2-Fluoro-α-cyclopropyl carbonylbenzyl bromide, 5,6,7,7a 
Tetrahydro-4H- theino-[3,2-c]- pyridone-2 p-toluenesulfonate and its hydrochloride salt.
