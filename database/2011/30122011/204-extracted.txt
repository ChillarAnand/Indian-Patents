(57) Abstract : 
The present invention provides a process for the preparation of the dextrorotatory isomer of zopiclone (eszopiclone). The present 
invention also provides eszopiclone di-p-anisolyl-L-tartrate and eszopiclone diacetyl-L-tartrate, which are useful as intermediates in a 
process for preparing eszopiclone.
