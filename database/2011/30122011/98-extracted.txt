(57) Abstract : 
The present invention relates to a brush filament for industrial applications and a brush made with the same. The brush filament 
comprises a matrix resin, a hydrolytic stabilizer and/or an acid-absorbing agent, and an antioxidant. The brush filament of the 
invention is suitable for industrial applications, in particular, for grinding, polishing and cleaning of marbles and/or metals after they 
have been cut.
