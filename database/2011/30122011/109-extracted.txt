(57) Abstract : 
A filter apparatus comprising: a vessel; a walnut shell filter media positioned in the vessel; a feed inlet positioned in the vessel and 
above the filter media; a draft tube system positioned in the filter media constructed and arranged to substantially roll the filter media; 
a first fluid inlet constructed and arranged to deliver a first fluid to the draft tube system; and a filtrate outlet positioned below the 
filter media.
