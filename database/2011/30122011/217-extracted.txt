(57) Abstract : 
The present invention relates to an imaging apparatus comprising an elongated carrier adapted to be inserted through a proximal 
opening of a gastrointestinal (GI) tract lumen; a piston head, coupled to a distal portion of the carrier, and configured to: be inflated so 
as to form and maintain a pressure seal with a wall of the GI tract lumen, and be advanced distally through the GI tract in response to 
pressure from a fluid pressure source; a distal balloon coupled to the carrier distal to the piston head and configured and operable to be 
inflated so as to dilate the lumen thereby creating a working space; and a control unit, configured and operable to control
