(57) Abstract : 
Assemblies and methods suitable for knotless arthroscopic repair of tissue defects include two fixation members coupled by two limbs 
of suture comprising a continuous loop. A unidirectional restriction element that can be a preformed locking, sliding suture knot 
proximate to one of the fixation members, provides tensioning of the repair.
