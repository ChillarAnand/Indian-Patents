(57) Abstract : 
A method for switching between channels on different bands is described. Communication with a wireless device occurs on a base 
channel. A channel switch request is sent to the wireless device. An acknowledgment is received from the wireless device. 
Communication with the wireless device switches to a 60 GHz channel.
