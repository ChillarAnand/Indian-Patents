(57) Abstract : 
The present invention relates to the pharmaceutical composition comprising the combination of lornoxicam and diacerein, which 
provide the patients a better and faster relief from osteoarthritic pain. The composition can be applied to symptomatic treatments of 
various febrile illnesses, various arthritises and inflammatory rheumatic diseases, soft tissue rheumatisms, neuralgia, arthralgia and 
pains, wounds relevant to degenerative diseases of spines, or postoperative pains.
