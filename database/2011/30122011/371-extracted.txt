(57) Abstract : 
The invention relates to a rubber mixture comprising at least one rubber and at least one low-PAH-content carbon black, where the 
low-PAH-content carbon black has (A) an iodine number of from 17 to 75 mg/g, (B) an STSA surface area of from 17 to 64 m2/g, (C) 
an iodine number:STSA surface area ratio > 1.06 mg/m2, (D) an OAN number of from 60 to 160 ml/100 g, (E) a COAN number of 
from 40 to 110 ml/100 g, (F) a mode greater than 100 nm and (G) benzo(a)pyrene content < 2 ppm. The rubber mixture can be used to 
produce technical rubber items and tyres.
