(57) Abstract : 
The disclosure relates to a school architecture aimed at utilizing maximum benefit from natural resources so as to make the school 
architecture compliant with green buildings. In an embodiment, the green school architecture comprises of configuring the mode of 
learning and teaching multiple subject matters non-renewable resource independent and at the same time increasing the effectiveness 
of imparting education.
