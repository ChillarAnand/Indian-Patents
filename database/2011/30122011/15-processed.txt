Abstract  =>  The present disclosure relates to a wheelchair for transportation of disabled person. The wheelchair comprises a chassis plurality of wheels fixed to either end of chassis. Atleast one vertical support member mounted at predetermined location on the chassis to support a plurality of tubular fixator. A seat is joined to the tubular fixator which is pivoted onto the vertical support member and plurality of friction knobs are provided on the tubular fixator to lock the horizontal and vertical movement of tubular fixator.
