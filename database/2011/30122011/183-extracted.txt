(57) Abstract : 
A method for preparing a whole grain beverage via steam injection is described. More particularly, the method includes the steps of 
dispersing whole grain flour in a product batch to form a product mixture and subjecting the product mixture to steam injection to 
obtain a whole grain beverage. The whole grain beverage produced by this method has overall enhanced stability.
