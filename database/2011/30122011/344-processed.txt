Abstract  =>  Multimedia items are selected from a plurality of candidate multimedia items by: determining (201) a plurality of features characterizing a user collection of multimedia items; determining (203) a probability function from said determined features, said probability function having a plurality of maxima, said plurality of maxima indicating the probability that a user prefers an item having the combination of features represented by said maxima; and selecting (209) at least one multimedia item from a plurality of candidate multimedia items on the basis of at least one of said determined maxima.
