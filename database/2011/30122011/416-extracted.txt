(57) Abstract : 
A modular test plug assembly is disclosed having a design which may be arranged in a plurality of different configurations. The test 
plug assembly includes a plurality of modules, having blades for insertion into a test switch assembly. The modules are positioned in a 
stacked arrangement and are secured together by end plates and a rod extending therebetween.
