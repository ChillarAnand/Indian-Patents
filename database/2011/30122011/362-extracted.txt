(57) Abstract : 
Provided are compositions for repelling fluids comprising a volatile liquid carrier, a powder-feel agent and less than 5 weight % of an 
ester selected from the group consisting of formula I, formula II, formula III, and combinations of two or more thereof: wherein R1, 
R2, R3, R5, R6, R8 and R9 are independently linear or branched, substituted or unsubstituted, saturated or unsaturated, C3-C22 alkyl 
or alkenyl groups, R4 is a linear or branched, substituted or unsubstituted, saturated or unsaturated, C3-C22 alkylene or alkenylene 
moiety, and R7 is a linear or branched, substituted or unsubstituted, saturated or unsaturated C3-C22 moiety, the composition being 
substantially anhydrous. Also provided are methods of improving dryness and/or comfort associated with the intimate area, as well as, 
kits comprising the composition and an absorbent article.
