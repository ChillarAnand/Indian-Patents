(57) Abstract : 
The invention relates to a device for extinguishing a fire which is suitable for carrying and handling by persons, comprising: - a 
pressureless container for placing of an extinguishing agent in solid form; - an ignition member for electrical or thermal ignition of the 
extinguishing agent for the purpose of forming aerosol during extinguishing operations; - a discharge member for discharge of the 
aerosol in a directed jet or plume; - a handling member for handling of the device by a user; and - a communication member for 
remote communication.
