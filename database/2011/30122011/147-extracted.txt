(57) Abstract : 
A method for making a genetically modified cell having the ability to produce fucosylated compounds comprising the steps of - 
transforming the cell to express a fucose kinase - transforming the cell to express a fucose-1-phosphate guanylyltransferase - 
transforming the cell to express a fucosyltransferase.
