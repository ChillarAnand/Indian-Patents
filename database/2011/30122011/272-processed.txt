Abstract  =>  The present invention relates to telecommunications, and particularly, to technology of generating codewords and determining an information symbol from a codeword for transmission in a telecommunication system. The method and apparatus provided by the embodiments of the invention allow up to a maximum number of codewords to be generated given the code alphabet size. Embodiments of the invention also provide method and apparatus for determining an information symbol from a codeword.
