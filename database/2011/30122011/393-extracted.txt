(57) Abstract : 
A method of individually packaging an absorbent article and attaching the absorbent article to an undergarment including the steps of 
providing a tri-folded absorbent article having two end portions and an intermediate portion, providing a pouch containing the tri-
folded absorbent article, opening the pouch to thereby expose a garment-facing surface of the intermediate portion of the absorbent 
article prior to exposing a garment facing surface of either of the end portions of the absorbent article.
