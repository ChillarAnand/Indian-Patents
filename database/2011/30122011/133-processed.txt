Abstract  =>  A process for the manufacture of clopidogrel oral dosage forms comprising the following steps: a. Compacting the active ingredient to obtain a granulate having a bulk density of 0.2 g/cc to 0.65 g/cc. b. Dry mixing the granulate obtained with at least one pharmaceutically acceptable ingredient c. Processing the blend so obtained in step b into a solid unitary dosage form.
