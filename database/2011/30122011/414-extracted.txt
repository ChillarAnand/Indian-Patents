(57) Abstract : 
An anti-wear layer 12 is provided on a textile tool 1, said anti-wear layer extending over a cutting edge 9 or a point 5. The point 5 or 
cutting edge 9 is provided with a cap 7 or 11 consisting of a protective material that preferably is configured so as to be viscously 
plastic and have a damping character. Preferably, the cap 7, 11 can be easily removed. For example, during the first use of the textile 
tool, said cap detaches as a whole or in parts from the textile tool 1. Preferably, the protective material is a melt-immersion material, 
for example, on a thermoplastic basis.
