(57) Abstract : 
A sanitant component comprising a sanitant to sanitise at least a part of a water purification apparatus and/or a water distribution 
apparatus and one or more sanitant receivers to receive residual sanitant and/or sanitant products from the water purification apparatus 
and/or water distribution apparatus. In this way, residual sanitant and/or sanitant products from the water purification apparatus and/or 
water distribution apparatus can be conveniently collected back into the sanitising component for easier, and/or quick and/or more 
convenient disposal.
