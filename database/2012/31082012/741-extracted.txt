(57) Abstract : 
An oral solid dosage form containing nanoparticles is made by (a) reducing the particle size of at least one pharmaceutically active 
ingredient dispersed in a solution containing fish gelatin to form a nanosuspension and (b) freeze-drying the nanosuspension of step 
(a) to form the oral solid dosage form.
