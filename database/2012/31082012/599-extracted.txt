(57) Abstract : 
The present invention relates to vaccine(s) comprising cancer cells expressing antigen(s), excipients, optionally adjuvant wherein the 
said antigen(s) is expressed on contacting the said cancer cell with p38 inducer, for use in treatment of Cancer. The vaccine 
composition induces specific immune response against homologous and heterologus cancer cells of the tissue /organ. The invention 
also provides method of preparing the same.
