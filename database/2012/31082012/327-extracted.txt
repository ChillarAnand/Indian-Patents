(57) Abstract : 
The present invention provides a planetary wheel grinding machine for grinding bars. The machine grinds round bars online. Further, 
the machine and the process achieve heavy stock removal and at feed rates of 15M/min to lOOM/mm. The machine includes a rotating 
drum, a grinding wheel and a wheel wear compensation assembly for moving the grinding wheel on a pivoted bracket after wearing 
thereby maintaining pressure on the bar.
