A method and system for sorting sperm samples according to different levels of DNA fragmentation and methods of using populations 
with low levels of DNA fragmentation to improve fertility and success rates of assisted reproductive procedures, including artificial 
insemination, in vitro fertilization, intracytoplasmic injection, and other related techniques.
