(57) Abstract : 
An equipment cabinet (100) having an air cooling system comprising: an air inlet (101) extending across an outer wall (102) of the 
cabinet; an air inlet filter (103) disposed between opposing first and second side panels (104, 105) forming the outer wall of the 
cabinet; and a fan (106) arranged to draw air through the filter and into an internal volume (107) of the cabinet for cooling equipment 
(108) contained therein.
