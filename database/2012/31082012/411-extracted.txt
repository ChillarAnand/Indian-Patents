(57) Abstract : 
A method for reducing halide concentration in a hydrocarbon product having an organic halide content which is made by a 
hydrocarbon conversion process using a halogen-containing acidic ionic liquid catalyst comprising contacting at least a portion of the 
hydrocarbon product with an aqueous caustic solution under conditions to reduce the halide concentration in the hydrocarbon product 
is disclosed.
