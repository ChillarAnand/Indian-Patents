(57) Abstract : 
An abrasive article includes an elongated body, a bonding layer including a metal overlying a surface of the elongated body, and a 
coating layer including a polymer material overlying the boding layer. The abrasive article further includes abrasive grains contained 
within the bonding layer and coating layer, and wherein the bonding layer comprises an average thickness (tbl) at least about 40% of 
the average grit size of the abrasive grains.
