(57) Abstract : 
A submersible electric pump includes a housing adapted to be submerged within a fluid to be pumped having apertures extending 
therethrough. An electric motor stator is positioned within the housing in communication with the apertures and is adapted to be in 
contact with the fluid. Inner and outer pump rotors are positioned within the housing in meshed engagement with one another to pump 
fluid when rotated. A plurality of permanent magnets are fixed for rotation with the outer rotor.
