(57) Abstract : 
A vaginal dilation device is provided that may include any of a number of features. One feature of the vaginal dilation device is that it 
is configured to dilate vaginal tissue during labor to prevent tissue damage. Another feature of the vaginal dilation device is that it can 
be manually controlled to dilate vaginal tissue, or can be automatically controlled to dilate vaginal tissue. In some embodiments, the 
vaginal dilation device is configured to measure a force applied by the device to tissue. In other embodiments, the vaginal dilation 
device is configured to apply a constant force to tissue. In other embodiments, the vaginal dilation device is configured to expand at a 
constant rate. Methods associated with use of the vaginal dilation device are also provided.
