(57) Abstract : 
An implant for insertion into the disc space between vertebrae. The implant including a spacer portion, a plate portion coupled to the 
spacer portion, a plurality of bone fixation elements for engaging the vertebrae and a retention mechanism for preventing the bone 
fixation elements from postoperatively uncoupling from the implant.
