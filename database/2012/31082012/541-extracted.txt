(57) Abstract : 
The present subject matter relates to method and systems for automatic switching of channels in a user device (110). The present 
subject matter includes determining occurrence of a primary switching event during reception of a current broadcast signal. Based on 
the determining, at least one broadcast signal from among a plurality of preferred broadcast signals is identified. The preferred 
broadcast signals are selected based in part on channel access pattern associated with a user. Further, a signal fingerprint of the at least 
one identified broadcast signal is compared with a plurality of reference signal fingerprints. Based on the comparison, a switching 
action is performed.
