(57) Abstract : 
An electrical terminal operable to facilitate electrical connectivity between the terminal and an electrical connector. The electrical 
terminal may include a cap to facilitate positioning a conducting element, such as but not limited to a coil spring, within a receptacle 
used to connect to an electrically conducting connector. The conducting element may facilitate electrical connectivity between the 
inserted connector and the terminal.
