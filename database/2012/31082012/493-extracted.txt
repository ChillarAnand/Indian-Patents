Compositions, recombinant vaccines and live attenuated pathogens comprising one or more isolated nucleic acid molecules that 
encode an immunogen in combination with an isolated nucleic acid molecule that encodes IL- 15Ra or a functional fragment thereof 
are disclosed. Methods of inducing an immune response in an individual against an immunogen, using such compositions are 
disclosed.
