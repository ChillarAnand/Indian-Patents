Abstract  =>  A tooth retention device (16) for attachment to an excavator bucket (10), which has a tooth mounting portion (20), a fork shaped body (22, 24) fitting over the edge (12) of the bucket (10), a clamp (32) passing through the body (22, 24) and the bucket (10), and a wedge (38) holding the clamp (32) in position, in which the wedge has a threaded rod (46) and (72) and a threaded block (50) on the rod (68). The rod (68) is rotatable to move the block (50) between locked and released positions.
