The invention provides solution compositions comprising olopatadine and a PDE4 inhibitor compound of Formula (I). The invention 
also provides methods for treating allergic and inflammatory diseases. More particularly, the present invention relates to formulations 
of olopatadine and their use for treating and/or preventing allergic or inflammatory disorders of the eye, nose, skin, and ear.
