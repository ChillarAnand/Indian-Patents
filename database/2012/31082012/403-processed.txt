Abstract  =>  The present invention discloses a method for implementing energy saving control at a NodeB, comprising: when a cell of a NodeB is required to sleep, a Radio Network Controller (RNC) sending an indication of sleeping a cell to the NodeB, and the NodeB sleeping the cell to enter into an energy saving state; and when the cell of the NodeB is required to wake up, the RNC sending an indication of waking up a cell to the NodeB, and the NodeB waking up the cell to exit the energy saving state. The present invention also discloses a system for implementing energy saving control at a NodeB.
