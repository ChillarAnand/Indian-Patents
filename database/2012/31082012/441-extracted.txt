(57) Abstract : 
Embodiments of the invention relate generally to the use of compositions comprising methylsulfonylmethane (MSM), and one or 
more therapeutic agents, for the treatment of drug-sensitive and drug resistant microorganisms. In several embodiments, such 
compositions are effective in treating drug resistant infectious diseases, for example, MRSA.
