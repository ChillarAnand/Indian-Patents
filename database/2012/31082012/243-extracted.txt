(57) Abstract : 
A synthetic alumino silicate zeolite catalyst containing at least one catalytically active transition metal selected from the group 
consisting of Cu, Fe, Hf, La, Au, In, V, lanthanides and Group VIII transition metals, which alumino silicate zeolite is a small pore 
aluminosilicate zeolite having a maximum ring size of eight tetrahedral atoms, wherein the mean crystallite size of the aluminosilicate 
zeolite determined by scanning electron microscope is >0.50 micrometer.
