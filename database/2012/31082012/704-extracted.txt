(57) Abstract : 
This invention relates to novel purified compounds of Formula (I). The invention includes all stereoisomeric forms and all tautomeric 
forms of the compounds of Formula (I) and pharmaceutically acceptable salts and derivatives. The present invention further relates to 
processes for the production of the novel antibacterial compounds by fermentation of the microorganism belonging to Streptomyces 
species (PM0626271 /MTCC 5447) and to pharmaceutical compositions containing one or more of the novel compounds as active 
ingredient and their use in medicines for treatment and prevention of diseases caused by bacterial infections.
