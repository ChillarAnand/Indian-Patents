(57) Abstract : 
Disclosed herein are functionalized electrodes and biosensors that can be used to detect biomolecules, such as a target analyte. In some 
embodiments, a functionalized electrode includes an electrically conducting surface, a first thiol compound and a second thiol 
compound. Also provided are kits and biosensor arrays including one or more disclosed functionalized electrodes and/or biosensors. 
Moreover, systems and methods for detecting biomolecules, such as a target analyte, with the disclosed functionalized electrodes 
and/or biosensors are also provided.
