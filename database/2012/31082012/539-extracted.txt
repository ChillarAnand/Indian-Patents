(57) Abstract : 
Compounds that are central nervous system drug candidates for the treatment of cognitive decline and, more particularly, Alzheimers 
disease are provided. Methods of treating, inhibiting, and/or abatement of cognitive decline and/or Alzheimers disease with a 
compound or pharmaceutically acceptable salt of the invention are also provided. Also provided are methods of preparing the 
compounds/compositions of the invention.
