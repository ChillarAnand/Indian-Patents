(57) Abstract : 
An air filter unit (20) configured for attachment to an external wall of an enclosure, the air filter unit comprising an inlet (21 ) at a base 
of the unit; a filter (23) formed by a passageway extending from the inlet and lined with bristles extending across the passageway 
along at least a portion of the passageway length; an outlet (28); and a heat exchanger (24) disposed between the filter and the outlet 
such that air passing through the inlet and filter passes through the heat exchanger prior to exiting the unit through the outlet, wherein 
the air filter unit is configured for attachment such that the heat exchanger is disposed above the filter in use.
