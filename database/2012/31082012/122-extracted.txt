(57) Abstract : 
The invention relates to a phosphated polycondensate which can be used as a plasticizer for hydraulic binders, containing a structural 
unit (I) having an aromatic or heteroaromatic and a polyether side chain, a phosphated structural unit (II) having an aromatic or 
heteroaromatic and a structural unit (III) having an aromatic or heteroaromatic, structural unit (II) and structural unit (III) differing 
exclusively in that the OP(OH)2 group of the structural unit (II) is replaced by H in structural unit (III), and structural unit (III) is not 
the same as structural unit (I).
