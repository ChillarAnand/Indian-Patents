Abstract  =>  The invention provides a system for installing a roof penetrating structure to a metal roof, the system comprising a) a rail and closure structure adapted to be supported by adjacent rib elevation of said roof; b) a skylight adapted to be supported on the rail and closure structure; and c) a support member for sealing a cut away portion of the rib structure to divert water away from the rail and closure structure.
