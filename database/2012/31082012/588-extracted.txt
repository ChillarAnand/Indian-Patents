(57) Abstract : 
Wall mounted speed regulator for a BLDC motor driven ceiling fan. The regulator (1) comprises an AC-DC converter (2) mounted on 
a wall and connected to an AC supply (3) and to a DC controller (4) provided with a speed controller knob (5). A DC-AC converter 
(6) is mounted in the housing of the BLDC motor (7) and connected to the AC-DC converter by cables (8) and to the BLDC motor by 
cables (9)
