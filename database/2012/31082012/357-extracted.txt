(57) Abstract : 
The process for crystal modification of Hydraziniijm Nitroformate (HNF) to reduce the aspect ratio (L/D), and reduced sensitivity 
comprising the steps of: a) preparing modified crystals of HNF with controlled shape and size; b) preparing precured polyurethane 
based nano-composite for coating of said modified HNF; c) coating of the modified crystals with the polyurethane based 
nanocomposite based on polyols.
