(57) Abstract : 
The technique disclosed herein includes a method and apparatus for controlling streamer steering devices to maintain a coil streamer 
shape that gives coverage for a coil shooting plan. The technique uses solved positions and a target coil streamer shape identified in 
the shooting plan to determine steering instructions to the streamer steering devices along the streamer.
