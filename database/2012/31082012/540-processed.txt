Abstract  =>  Disclosed is a sunscreen cosmetic comprising (1) a UVA absorbent selected from one or more of diethylaminohydroxybenzoyl hexyl benzoate, 2,4-bis{[4-(2-ethylhexyloxy)-2-hydroxy]-phenyl}-6-(4-methoxyphenyl)-1,3,5-triazine, 4-tert-butyl-4- methoxydibenzoylmethane, and 2-hydroxy-4-methoxybenzophenone and (2) spherical resin powder internally containing 41% or more of hydrophobized titanium oxide ultrafine particles. More specifically disclosed is a sunscreen cosmetic, comprising a UVA absorbent and titanium oxide ultrafine particles, which prevents staining due to the secondary adhesion thereof to clothes.
