Abstract  =>  The present invention provides a preload adjustment mechanism for front fork of a; vehicle. The mechanism includes an adjuster means having a cam profile a plunger rod being mounted within the adjuster means and a bolt cap for holding theplunger rod against linear and rotational pressure. Upon giving rotational motion to the plunger rod, a protrusion of the plunger rod slides over the cam profile thereby! achieving a predefined preload level.
