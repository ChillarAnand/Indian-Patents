(57) Abstract : 
The present invention relates to a predominantly soap based bar (e.g. about 50% to 85% by weight soap) specifically a toilet bar used 
for personal cleansing. In particular it relates to a toilet bar soap which has a specific formulation to take care of sensory attributes 
(e.g., skin hydration, skin softness, skin after-feel, rinsability & lather) during the washing process in soft and very soft water.
