(57) Abstract : 
The present invention aims to provide an eye drop for treating macular edema. The present invention provides an eye drop for treating 
macular edema, which contains difluprednate as an active ingredient. The eye drop can afford effects such as improvement of visual 
acuity and decreased foveal retinal thickness in macular edema.
