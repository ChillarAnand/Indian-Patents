(57) Abstract : 
The instant invention provides an isocyanate trimerisation catalyst system, a precursor formulation, a process for trimerising 
isocyanates, rigid foams made therefrom, and a process for making such foams. The trimerisation catalyst system comprises: (a) a 
phosphatrane cation; and (b) an isocyanate-trimer inducing anion; wherein said trimerisation catalyst system has a trimerisation 
activation temperature in the range of equal to or less than 73C.
