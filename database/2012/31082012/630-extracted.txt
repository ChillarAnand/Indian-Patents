(57) Abstract : 
The application describes a method for the preparation of 2,5-furandicarboxylic acid (FDCA) and/or an alkyl ester of FDCA 
comprising the step of contacting a feed comprising a starting material selected from 5-alkoxymethylfurfural, 2,5-
di(alkoxymethyl)furan and a mixture thereof with an oxidant in the presence of an oxidation catalyst. The feed may also comprise 5-
hydroxymethylfurfural as a further starting material.
