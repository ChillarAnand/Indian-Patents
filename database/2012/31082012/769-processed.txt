Abstract  =>  A draft device (1) of a drafting machine or lap-forming machine on a spinning line comprises an opening unit (10) positioned upline of the draft unit (2). The opening unit (2) comprises opening cylinders with annular bosses or pairs of overlapping cylinders with a cylindrical helix, suitable to sink into each sliver entering the unit to open it, improving the conditions for subsequent drafting.
