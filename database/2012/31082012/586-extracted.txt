(57) Abstract : 
The present invention relates to an asynchronous generator system for a wind turbine, and a wind turbine having such a system, and 
the method for operating and starting up such a wind turbine. The asynchronous generator system is thereby designed very simply and 
thus inexpensively and is able to absorb wind gusts and the associated speed increases.
