(57) Abstract : 
The present invention relates to an emulsion comprising a particle comprising a copolymer of polyethylene terephthalate and 
polyoxyethylene terephthalate and a silicone liquid and an aqueous continuous phase, a process to make the emulsion, to a laundry 
composition comprising the emulsion, and to the use of the copolymer to increase silicone deposition onto fabric.
