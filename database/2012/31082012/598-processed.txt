Abstract  =>  The present invention provides an arc chute assembly for a switching device. The arc chute assembly as of a plurality of differentially varied arc plates which are incrementally configured in the assembly. The assembly helps in achieving higher arc lengths and higher arc voltage. Achieving higher arc voltage helps the arc to quench faster.
