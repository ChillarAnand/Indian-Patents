(57) Abstract : 
The present disclosure relates to a purification medium. The said purification medium comprises a mixture of rice husk ash and clay 
including voids created by combustion of the mixture. The combustion of mixture causes removal of at least a fraction of carbon 
present in the rice husk ash.
