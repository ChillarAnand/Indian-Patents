(57) Abstract : 
A fastening apparatus of a combined boat for securely joining boat hulls of the combined boat is disclosed. Grooves (3) are provided 
at the peripheries of joints of the boat hulls. The fastening apparatus includes a holding band (2) with ridges (4) mating with the 
grooves (3), and the holding band engages via the ridges (4) with the grooves (3) so as to join the boat hulls of the combined boat 
together securely. The fastening apparatus of the combined boat is simple in structure, easy to manufacture, and convenient to mount 
and dismount.
