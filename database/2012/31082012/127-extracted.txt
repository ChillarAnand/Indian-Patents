(57) Abstract : 
A security laminate containing a dye diffusion transfer image receptor layer comprising a polymeric mordant for dyes on a support 
having a laser engraved first dye diffusion transfer image or a laser marked dye diffusion transfer image receptor layer in an area 
lacking the first dye diffusion transfer image. Methods for manufacturing and securing security document precusors are also disclosed.
