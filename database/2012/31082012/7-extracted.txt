(57) Abstract : 
Topical pharmaceutical compositions comprising shea butter, aloe butter, mango butter, cocoa butter, and one or more 
pharmaceutically acceptable excipients. Further, topical compositions comprising squalene, aloe vera, vitamin E acetate, and one or 
more pharmaceutically acceptable excipients is disclosed. These compositions are useful for the treatment of areas of skin of a person 
afflicted with psoriasis, dermatitis, skin disorders such as dry skin, eczema, itchy skin, red skin, inflamed and or cracked skin, severe 
dryness or scaling of palms, to provide a moisturizing effect to the skin.
