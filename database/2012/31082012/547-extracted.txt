(57) Abstract : 
The present invention relates to a system for effectively routing hydraulic fluid flow, controlled lowering of the implement using 
hydraulic system and obviating problems associated with leakages of hydraulic fluid inside the valve body, to enhance effectiveness 
and efficiency of the hydraulic system and thereby performance of the off-road vehicle such as tractor. The system comprises a valve 
block assembly, a microprocessor based controller, a double acting cylinder configured with a check valve and pilot piston valve, 
wherein said valve block assembly comprises of position control and draft control solenoid valve those are electronically configured 
with the said controller and hydraulically configured with a hitch pump and a steering pump.
