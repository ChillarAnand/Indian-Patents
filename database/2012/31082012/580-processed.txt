Abstract  =>  The present invention relates to a termination pitch adjustable transmission shaft assembly for moving contact in lv switchgear products. The assembly comprises a cassette means, plurality of rotor means (1) and coupler means (13). The rotor means (1) comprises a male feature (5) and a female feature (6) adapted to be operatively engaged with an identical or substantially identical female feature (6) and male feature (5) of another rotor means respectively so as to form transmission shaft means of a switch. The coupler means (13) comprises a male feature (5) and a female feature (6) adapted to be operatively engaged with proximate pole means such that termination pitch adjustable transmission shaft being formed ensuring wide range of termination pitch.
