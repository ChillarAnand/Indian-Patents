(57) Abstract : 
The invention relates to transparent fire-resistant glass elements made from at least two panes, wherein a layer made from a UV-
curable material is arranged between each of said panes, to a method for the production thereof and to the use thereof as multiple glass 
elements in mobile units such as doors and windows or fixed as elements of wall or facades.
