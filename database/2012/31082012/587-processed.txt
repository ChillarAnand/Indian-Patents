Abstract  =>  The invention provides for a down-the-hole drill bit (11 ) assembly comprising a drill bit (11 ) having a screw-thread (7) at its anvil end, external splines on the drill shank (13) and cooperating splines (9) within a drill casing as well as a co-operating screw thread (7) carried by a component within the casing. The assembly is arranged to have engagement of the splines (9) with the screw threads (7) disengaged for driving the drill and to have the screw threads (7) engaged and splines (9) disengaged to permit removal of the bit from the assembly.
