(57) Abstract : 
Providing a fast channel change function in a digital television system by hierarchically modulating each channel to provide both high 
priority (long interleave) and low priority (short interleave) signals, wherein a receiver may rapidly demodulate and use information 
within the low priority signal of a new channel to more rapidly change to the new channel.
