Abstract  =>  The invention is deviced which can take smear from any type of cervix. Since it enters endocervical canal there is no need for separate brush to take endocervical smears. It is made up of wood. It can take good scraping of endo cervix and transformation zone also. It gives very good scrapes without bleeding. Newly devised spatula is sturdy & can withstand repeated sterilization & reuse cycles. New spatula saves on resources, increases efficiently & quality of smears.
