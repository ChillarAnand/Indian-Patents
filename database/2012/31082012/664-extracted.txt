(57) Abstract : 
The present invention concerns a biodegradable fibre comprising composite filaments of cellulose and cellulose acetate, and a process 
for making such a fibre comprising providing a solution dope comprising a blend of cellulose and cellulose acetate in an ionic liquid 
or in N-methylmorpholine-N-oxide (NMMO), and spinning casting the blend into a protic solvent to generate fibres. The invention 
also concerns materials made from such a fibre, and garments or soft furnishings made from such a material.
