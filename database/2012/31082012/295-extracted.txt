(57) Abstract : 
A system for in-place visualization of sensed data is provided. The system includes a formable sheet comprising a display and sensors 
embedded within the sheet underneath the display. The display will display information relating to sensed data on a portion of the 
display corresponding to locations of the sensors located underneath the display. As a result, the display displays the information 
above or directly above the sensors that output data.
