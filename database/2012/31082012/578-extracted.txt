(57) Abstract : 
Disclosed herein is a high concentrated liquid rectal spray composition comprising Bisacodyl and its pharmaceutically acceptable salts 
in unique blend of solvent, mucoadhesive polymers and preservatives; along with pharmaceutically acceptable excipients, useful for 
the treatment of constipation and bowel evacuation before investigational procedures or surgery. Also the invention discloses a 
process for preparation of said composition.
