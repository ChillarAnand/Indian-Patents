The belt (30) of a lap-forming device (1) is multilayer and has a processing layer (30a) destined to come into contact with the lap, 
made from polyurethane, an intermediate reinforcement layer (30b) made from polyester fabric and a dragging layer (30c) destined to 
come into contact with the primary calender (22), made from polyester.
