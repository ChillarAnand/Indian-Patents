(57) Abstract : 
The present invention relates to a process for production of pure terephthalic acid comprising: a) removing a mother liquor from a 
terephthalic acid through a filter with a gas, wherein the gas comprises steam at a concentration of least about 50% by volume; b) 
purifying the gas; and c) recycling the gas purified in step (b) back to step (a).
