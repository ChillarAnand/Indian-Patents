(57) Abstract : 
A wind turbine rotor blade is provided, the rotor blade comprising a root portion and a tip portion extending therefrom. The root 
portion comprises a plurality of inserts embedded therein, each insert being configured to receive respective connecting means for 
connecting the wind turbine rotor blade to a wind turbine hub. The inserts are of at least two different lengths such that they extend 
into the rotor blade by different distances.
