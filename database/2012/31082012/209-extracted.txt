(57) Abstract : 
A process for preparing a diaminoacetonitrile which includes reacting by contacting an amine comprising two primary amine groups 
with a cyanohydrin. The diaminoacetonitrile produced may subsequently be used in the production of polymers and/or as a curing 
agent for epoxy resins.
