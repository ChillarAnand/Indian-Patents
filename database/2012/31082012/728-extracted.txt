(57) Abstract : 
Therapeutics which employ a combination of an antiviral agent and an EP4 receptor agonist for the treatment of human respiratory 
diseases associated with viral infections are described. Viral infections may include an influenza A virus, for example H1N1, H3N2 
and H5N1, and mutations thereof, and/or a coronavirus, for example a virus that causes severe acute respiratory syndrome, SARS.
