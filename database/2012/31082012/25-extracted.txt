(57) Abstract : 
An electrical terminal operable to facilitate electrical connectivity between the terminal and an electrical connector. The electrical 
terminal may include a conducting element, such as but not limited to a coil spring, within a open end used to connect to an 
electrically conducting connector. The conducting element may facilitate electrical connectivity between the inserted connector and 
the terminal.
