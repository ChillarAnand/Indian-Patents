Abstract  =>  The invention relates to A plasma source excited by a high-frequency signal comprising a first rod (3) forming a quarter-wave antenna surrounded with at least one parallel rod forming a coupler (6, 7, 8), substantially of same length as the first rod, set to a reference voltage, the coupler- forming rods being regularly distributed, radially, around the first rod, at a distance varying from approximately half to one twentieth of the quarter of the wavelength, the antenna and the couplers being oriented in a same, direction.
