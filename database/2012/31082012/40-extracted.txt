(57) Abstract : 
The invention relates to a method of quality control testing a Factor XIII (FXIII) containing sample which comprises the step of 
detecting the presence of and/or measuring the concentration of pre-activated FXIII (FXIIIa0) in said sample and to a quality control 
kit for determining the quality of a Factor XIII (FXIII) containing sample. Preferably, an anion-exchange chromatographic column is 
used, as well as the fluorescent substrate Abz-NE (Cad-Dnp) EQVS PLTLLK-OH
