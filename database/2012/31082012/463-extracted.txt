A machine for harvesting and collecting cotton bolls comprising at least one vibrator unit at least one conveyor unit, at least one 
suction and blowing unit and at least one storage unit. The machine comprises a plurality of vibrating rods. The machine further 
comprises a plurality of blowers of suction and blowing unit are preferably arranged at both ends of machine.
