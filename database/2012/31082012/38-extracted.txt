(57) Abstract : 
The invention relates a method wherein a molecule is used for inducing and/or promoting skipping of at least one of exon 43, exon 46, 
exons 50-53 of the DMD pre- mRNA in a patient, preferably in an isolated cell of a patient, the method comprising providing said cell 
and/or said patient with a molecule. The invention also relates to said molecule as such.
