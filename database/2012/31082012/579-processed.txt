Abstract  =>  Disclosed herein is a liquid oral spray composition comprising combination of antiallergic, cough suppressant and nasal decongestant useful for management of dry or non-productive cough, cough associated with viral or bacterial inflammation of upper respiratory tract, sinusitis, laryngitis, tracheitis and pharyngitis. Also disclosed herein is a spray dispenser that delivers the said composition in the form of fine droplets for easy delivery even to the paediatrics.
