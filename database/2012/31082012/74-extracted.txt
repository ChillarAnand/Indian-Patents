(57) Abstract : 
Chewable compositions containing at least 20% of recommended daily allowance of water-soluble vitamins are used to maintain a 
beneficial amount of such vitamins in a consumers bloodstream throughout a day. Use of such chewable compositions may be 
supplemented by additional single dose intake of such water-soluble vitamins.
