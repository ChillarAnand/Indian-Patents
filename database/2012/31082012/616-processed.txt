Abstract  =>  An automatic silting-removing method for shaft sealing device of concrete mixer is provided, which is that, determining whether the silting exists by detecting the oil pressure in the lubrication passage (7) of an oil passage (320) and supplying air flow to the lubrication passage (7) to remove the silting when the determining result is positive. A device for applying the above method includes a shaft sealing part (310), an oil passage part (320), an air passage part (330) and a control part (340).
