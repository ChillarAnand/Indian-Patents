Abstract  =>  The invention relates to a monitoring system (1) which comprises at least one monitoring satellite (S2) placed in orbit (02) at a lower altitude than that of the satellite (S1) of the satellite constellation (2) so as to be capable to receiving the positioning signals emitted towards the Earth (T) by said satellites (S1) and which comprises a processing unit(11) intended for verifying the integrity of said received positioning signals, using position information that is separate from said signals for this purpose.
