(57) Abstract : 
Methods for preventing or reducing the formation of scale in a wet-process phosphoric acid production process by intermixing a 
water-soluble functional organic reagent with a phosphoric acid at one or more step of the phosphoric acid production process in an 
amount sufficient to prevent or reduce at least one species of scale are provided.
