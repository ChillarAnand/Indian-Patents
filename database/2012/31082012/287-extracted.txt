The invention relates to novel antimicrobial agents that are based on -lactam derivatives and are produced by reacting previously 
known -lactam derivatives with polyphenol oxidase substrates under the influence of free radicals and by forming salts of any -lactam 
derivatives with polyhexamethylene biguanide hydrogen carbonate. Said novel compounds are suitable as an antibiotic.
