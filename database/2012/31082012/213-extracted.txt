(57) Abstract : 
The invention relates to a method for providing a trigger signal in response to the commutation of a mechanically commutated electric 
motor (1). The method comprising the steps of providing a mechanically commutated electric motor (1), providing a power supply for 
said mechanically commutated electric motor via electrical supply leads (10, 11) from power supply circuitry, providing a filter (15) 
connected to said electrical supply leads (10, 11), detecting with said filter (15) a voltage spike occurring at commutation, outputting 
from said filter (15) said trigger signal.
