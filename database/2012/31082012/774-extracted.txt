(57) Abstract : 
An abrasive article having an abrasive body including abrasive grains contained within a bond material, wherein the abrasive grains 
comprise microcrystalline alumina, and wherein the bond material includes less than about 1.0 mol% phosphorous oxide (P2O5), and 
a ratio measured in mol% between a total content of sodium oxide (Na2O) and a total content of potassium oxide (K2O) defined by 
[K2O/Na2O] having a value greater than about 0.5.
