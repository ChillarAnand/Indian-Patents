Abstract  =>  The present invention discloses a location method, apparatus and system, and the method comprises the following steps: a Mobility Management Entity (MME) receives a location service request, selects an Evolved Service Mobile Location Center (E-SMLC), and transmits the location service request including a location identifier to the selected E-SMLC; the MME receives a User Equipment (UE) specific message including the location identifier transmitted by the E-SMLC, and communicates the location information with the E-SMLC using the location identifier. The embodiments of the present invention can ensure the correct delivery of the location information, and further improve the correctness of the location technology.
