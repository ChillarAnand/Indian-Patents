Abstract  =>  The present invention relates to a process of manufacturing bi-axially oriented polyester film directly from the polymer melt. More particularly, the said process minimises the waste generated in the process and/or to reuse the waste. The present process is capable to produce single or multilayer bi-axially oriented polyester film, having uniform thickness.
