Abstract  =>  The present invention relates to a polymer material which is a copolymer of polyethylene terephthalate and polyoxyethylene terephthalate modified by attachment of a silicone group which is covalently bonded either directly or via an optional linker group to the copolymer, to an emulsion comprising a particle comprising (a) the modified copolymer and (b) a silicone liquid, and an aqueous continuous phase, a process to make the emulsion, to a laundry composition comprising the modified polymer or emulsion, and to the use of the modified copolymer to increase silicone deposition onto fabric.
