(57) Abstract : 
Methods and compositions for modulating phytopathogenic infection in a plant comprise applying to the plant and/or seeds thereof 
and/or substrate used for growing said plant an amount of a sarmentine and/or a sarmentine analog having substantially the same 
activity as sarmentine effective to modulate said phytopathogenic infection via virus, fungi, bacteria, nematode and insect.
