(57) Abstract : 
Described herein are compounds that are antagonists of lysophosphatidic receptor(s). Also described are pharmaceutical compositions 
and medicaments that include the compounds described herein, as well as methods of using such antagonists, alone and in 
combination with other compounds, for treating LPA-dependent or LPA-mediated conditions or diseases.
