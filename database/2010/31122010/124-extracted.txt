(57) Abstract : 
Pharmaceutical compositions comprising phenylephrine or a pharmaceutically acceptable salt thereof and methods for administering 
the pharmaceutical compositions wherein the composition is formulated for systemic absorption of phenylephrine that avoids first pass 
metabolism. The compositions of the invention are formulated to be applied to oral mucosa of an animal to allow for enhanced 
systemic delivery of therapeutically active form of phenylephrine.
