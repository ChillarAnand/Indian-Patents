(57) Abstract : 
A method of purifying arsenic contaminated water is disclosed. The method  comprises of adding to the arsenic contaminated water 
predetermined quantities of iron and alkali; and passing the water so obtained through a filter medium including rice husk ash. A 
device for purification of arsenic contaminated water is also disclosed.
