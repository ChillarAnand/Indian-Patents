(57) Abstract : 
The invention relates to a SIM card (21) or chip card (20), particularly for authenticating at least one mobile terminal, wherein the 
SIM card (21) comprises at least one processor (1) and contact areas (22) for connecting the SIM card (21) to a mobile terminal, 
wherein the SIM card (21) comprises an antenna (15, 23) or comprises further contact areas (14, 24) for connecting to an antenna (15, 
23), and that the SIM card (21) comprises a radio interface (200) for establishing and maintaining a short-range radio connection.
