(57) Abstract : 
A method of transmitting uplink signals is disclosed. The method includes transmitting, by a user equipment, periodic control 
information on a Physical Uplink Control Channel (PUCCH) at a predetermined period, dropping the periodic control information and 
multiplexing uplink signals except the periodic control information when the user equipment is operating in subframe bundling 
transmission mode where the uplink signals are transmitted in a plurality of consecutive sub frames, and transmitting the multiplexed 
uplink signals on a Physical Uplink Shared Channel (PUSCH).
