Abstract  =>  The embodiments of the present invention disclose a method of pathfinding on a map, including: dividing a plane map into at least two areas of the same size in advance, and determining a path point on a border of the areas; finding a path by taking the area as an unit when the path needs to be found, and obtaining an area path; and performing pathfinding based on a triangular facet in each area according to the area path, and generating an ultimate path.
