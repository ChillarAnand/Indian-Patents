Abstract  =>  A scraper for scraping and/or cleaning, especially for scraping and/or cleaning of units which serve the production and/or treatment of a fibrous web and preferably for scraping and/or cleaning of drying cylinders is composed of a composite material comprising a resin matrix material with fibers embedded therein. Copper and/or at least one copper alloy are mixed into the resin matrix material, and/or the resin matrix material is substituted at least partially by silicone modified epoxy resin.
