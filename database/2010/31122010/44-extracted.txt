(57) Abstract : 
The invention relates to an oil pan (1) for an internal combustion engine, comprising a trough-shaped body (2) which can be fastened 
with a multi-part flange-like frame to a crankcase. In order to reduce the noise emissions of the oil pan (1) in the simplest possible 
way, it is provided that the frame parts (3a, 3b) are arranged at a distance from one another in the longitudinal direction (x) of the 
frame (3).
