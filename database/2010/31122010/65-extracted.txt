(57) Abstract : 
The present invention relates to a system of cladding of tiles in different sizes on exterior or interior of load bearing walls of buildings 
and other structures. More particularly, invention is directed to ventilated /external cladding wall system comprising selective extruded 
metal sections, runners, interlocks, clamps etc and the said tiles in an assembly for such cladding safely, securely attached to a backing 
/support wall. Advantageously the system is adapted to be hung on horizontal runner/interlocking members of selective aluminium 
extruded sections adapted to support to securely retain the tiles on said support structures. More advantageously the present invention 
is capable of wide scale household/commercial or industrial application in walls of buildings and structures as well as for roof and 
sloping portions.
