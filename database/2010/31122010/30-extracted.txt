(57) Abstract : 
The present invention is a cosmetic composition for skin care that has one or more plant active metabolites/extracts, one or more 
carrier oil/s and at least one emulsifier, and one or more antioxidant/s. The composition is a stable oil-in-water emulsion and provides 
a soothing effect besides protection from UV A and UV B for all types of skin.
