(57) Abstract : 
A lens system suitable for use with a camera is disclosed. The lens system employs liquid optics to provide stabilization of an image. 
A pair of liquid lens cells provides stabilization of the image. A second pair of liquid lens cells may provide stabilization in another 
direction. The two pairs of liquid cells may provide stabilization in any direction.
