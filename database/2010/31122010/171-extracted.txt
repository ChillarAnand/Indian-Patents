(57) Abstract : 
A ceiling fan including a connecting fitting fixed to a ceiling, and a ceiling fan main body suspended from the connecting fitting via a 
suspension device. The ceiling fan main body includes a motor for rotating a plurality of blades attached in a horizontal direction, a 
shaft protruding to an upper part of the motor, and a joint portion fixed on an upper part of the shaft. The suspension device includes a 
first connecting portion capable of being movably mounted on the connecting fitting, and a second connecting portion located in a 
lower part of the first connecting portion. The joint portion is movably mounted on the second connecting portion.
