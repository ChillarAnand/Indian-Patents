(57) Abstract : 
In this paper design and development of Gate Operated Repulsive Magnetic Piston Fuel Free Engine hereafter called Gate Operated 
Piston Engine or GOPI Engine or Engine is presented. This engine works on the principle of repulsive force of magnets. The engine 
can be used to produce electrical and/or mechanical output with very high efficiency. The engine is producing zero pollution as it is 
not using any type of fuel. For start of the engine some kind of storage energy as in the form of battery is required.
