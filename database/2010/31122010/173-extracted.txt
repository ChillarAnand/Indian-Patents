(57) Abstract : 
The invention relates to a valve plate (10) comprising a cooling medium duct (20) for a compressor (14) used for generating 
compressed air. According to the invention, from the perspective of a piston chamber (40) of the compressor (14), at least part of the 
cooling medium duct (20) extends between the piston chamber (40) and an air discharge valve (22, 24) that is arranged in the valve 
plate (10) . The invention further relates to a method for cooling compressed air in a valve plate (10) of a compressor (14).
