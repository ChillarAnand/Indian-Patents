(57) Abstract : 
The invention relates to an open implant constituted of an osteogenic composition comprising at least: · one osteogenic growth 
factor/amphiphilic anionic polysaccharide complex, • one soluble salt of a cation at least divalent, and • one organic support, • said 
organic support comprising no demineralized bone matrix. In one embodiment, said implant is in the form of a lyophilizate. It also 
relates to the method for the preparation thereof.
