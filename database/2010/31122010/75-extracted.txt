(57) Abstract : 
There is provided a reduced dose pharmaceutical composition in the form of supersaturable self-emulsifying drug delivery system 
comprising celecoxib or salts thereof wherein the dose of the celecoxib is 120-185mg. The composition of the present invention may 
exhibit improved bioavailability along with reduced undesirable side effects. The invention also relates to processes for the 
preparation of such compositions.
