(57) Abstract : 
The invention relates to a bush widening apparatus and a process by means of which it is possible, with relatively low investment 
costs, to partially forge certain sections of large and heavy bush blanks (5) by means of a motor-driven, e.g. hydraulically driven, 
forming tool (4), both the diameter and the height of these blanks increasing in size.
