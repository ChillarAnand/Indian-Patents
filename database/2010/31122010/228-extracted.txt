(57) Abstract : 
Systems and methods for the separation of a particulate-fluid suspension are provided. An apparatus for the separation of a particulate-
fluid suspension can include an enclosed vessel having two or more sections disposed coaxially along a common longitudinal 
centerline, wherein a first section has a first cross sectional area, and a second section has a second cross sectional area. A plurality of 
apertures can be disposed about the second section. The apparatus can have a cylindrical surface, parallel to the longitudinal centerline 
of the apparatus, disposed within the first section. A fluid distribution channel having a plurality of apertures can be disposed either 
about an exterior surface or an interior of the apparatus. A plurality of fluid conduits can provide fluid communication between the 
fluid distribution channel and the plurality of apertures distributed about the second section.
