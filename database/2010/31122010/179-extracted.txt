(57) Abstract : 
The invention relates to a method and equipment for treatment of black liquor at a pulp mill in order to recover chemicals and energy 
contained therein. In the invention, the black liquor is pyrolyzed in a pyrolysis reactor (4), the formed gaseous components are 
forwarded for utilization and the solids are mixed with water, whereby the formed solids are returned to a lime burning kiln (1) and the 
sodium-hydroxide-containing liquid is returned to the pulp cooking process.
