(57) Abstract : 
Disclosed herein is a system 10 including a motor 20 comprising a rotor 30, a stator 40 and a sealing assembly 100 having at least one 
joint and a monolithic ceramic separator 110. Each joint of the sealing assembly 100 is a chemical bond joint, and the monolithic 
ceramic separator 110 is disposed in a gap 50 between the rotor 30 and the stator 40 of the motor 20 such that the sealing assembly 
100 hermetically isolates the rotor 30 and the stator 40.
