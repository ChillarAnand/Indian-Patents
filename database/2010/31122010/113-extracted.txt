(57) Abstract : 
A vibrating and rotating prilling bucket (15) for granulation of a fluid substance (U), said bucket (15) comprising a perforated side 
wall (15a) and vibrating devices (51, 52) arranged to deliver a force with constant direction (A-A) . In a preferred embodiment, the 
vibrating unit comprise two counter-rotating vibrators delivering rotating forces which balance each other perpendicularly to said 
constant direction (A-A).
