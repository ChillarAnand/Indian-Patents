(57) Abstract : 
This invention relates to the field of polymeric materials that can be used in combination with a wide variety of substrates, such as 
personal care products, textiles, metal, cellulosic materials, plastics, and the like, and to the field of active agents including, for 
example, antimicrobial, antibacterial and antifungal materials. This invention further relates to latex polymer coatings that comprise at 
least one active component as well as methods for making and using such latex compositions.
