A powder treated with a (meth)acrylic-grafted silicone polymer, the treated powder being obtained from a mixture containing a 
volatile silicone oil, a (meth)acrylic-grafted silicone polymer in which a monomer component comprising a (meth)acrylic series 
monomer has been grafted onto the main chain of a polysiloxane, and a powder.
