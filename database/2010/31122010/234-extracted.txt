(57) Abstract : 
A method of supplying storage devices (500) to a storage device testing system (100) includes placing a storage device tote (600), 
carrying multiple storage devices (500), in a presentation position accessible to an automated transporter (200) of the storage device 
testing system. The method includes actuating the automated transporter to retrieve one of the storage devices from the storage device 
tote, and actuating the automated transporter to deliver the retrieved storage device to a test slot (310) of the storage device testing 
system and insert the storage device in the test slot.
