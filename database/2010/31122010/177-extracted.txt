(57) Abstract : 
A piston (10) for an internal combustion engine comprises at least one cooling channel (24) located only in the area of at least one 
inflow and at least one outflow (14) at a low level (12) comparatively removed from the base of the piston and at a constant higher 
level (16) closer to the piston base, said cooling channel comprising steep inclines (18) between the areas located at the low (12) and 
at the high level (16).
